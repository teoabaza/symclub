Would you like to join?

South Yorkshire Mountaineering Club

The club has been hiking and climbing in the Peak District and all over the world for 70 years.

We are an informal mountaineering club, happy to welcome new members or help passing climbers to find the best crags.

Prospective members

The Club caters for all, young and old, expert and novice, and we boast a broad range of interests and experience. You are welcome to join us.

Coronavirus guidance

All meets are to be conducted in accordance with the current government requirements (legal and guidance) for social distancing and some meets may have restricted numbers.

Comittee

Meet the people who make it possible and the first point of contact for people interested in joining the Club and contributing to our events.

Friday social

Every Friday at 3 p.m. we meet at the Depot Climbing wall in Sheffield for some indoor fun. Usually we meet again afterwards at the Red Deer pub for some drinks and more socialising.
